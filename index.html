Dear Mom ❤️

Mom, I may not always show it, but everything I am is because of you. You built my life with love, patience, and silent sacrifices.

You gave up your comfort so I could have mine. You carried my struggles even when I didn’t understand your pain.

I still wonder how you stayed strong on days when life was hardest, yet made sure I never felt even a moment of it.

If I ever become someone good in life, it will only be because I was raised by someone extraordinary — you.

No matter where life takes me, I will always find peace in you, because you are my home.

I hope one day I can give you even a fraction of the love you gave me every single day. Until then, I will just keep loving you endlessly.

Love You Forever ❤️
