Dear Mom ❤️

Mom, if I ever become even half the person I am meant to be, it will be because of you. You didn’t just raise me—you shaped my entire world with your love.

You stayed strong even when you were tired, smiled even when you were hurting, and gave me comfort even when you had none left for yourself.

I may not always say it, but I notice everything you do for me. Every sacrifice, every silent prayer, every moment you chose me over yourself.

No matter how far I go in life, no success, no place, no person will ever replace you. Home will always mean you.

Thank you for being my strength when I had none, and my peace when the world felt too loud.

Love You Forever ❤️
